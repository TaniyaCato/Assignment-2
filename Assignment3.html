Workouts to Improve Fitness Levels

-Endurance Training, helps develop stamina, and improve the functioning of the heart and lungs, it also helps people who are looking to losing weight, burn calories.

-Strength Training, known as resistance training. The resistance can come from using barbells, dumbbells, resistance bands, or strength machines. So this would be more for people who are trying to tone there body or gain weight.

-Static Stretching, to combat muscle inflexibility, benefits from static stretching include enhanced range of motion, stronger joints, and better physical performance if you play sports. So this is meant more for athletes.

Nutrition

When it comes to being healthy and fit, it isn't all about just working out, you also have to eat better for faster results. You need quality foods, protein, heart-healthy fats, and fluids. Your muscles rely on carbohydrate foods such as: breads, pastas, fruits, and vegetables. The only types of foods that you need to focus on before working out are foods that are: low fat, low fiber, and contain fluids. It is very important to drink plenty of water, water acts as your body's cooling system. Staying dehydrated is very important, especially when working out. Sport drinks are also very helpful, they give you enough carbs and sodiums, as well as fluids.
